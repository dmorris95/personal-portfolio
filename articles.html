AI and Meetings - January 2024

AI and Meetings

The article talks about how some AIs are capable of summarizing meetings and providing you notes on the meeting. While I think this could be useful in certain situations, it does not make up for a person actually being at the meeting and listening with their own ears. Picking up cadence and certain tones can be difficult for AI and a person would have a much easier time understanding the meeting by just being there.

Teaching a Computer to Type - April 2024

Teaching a Computer to Type

This article goes over how instead of a computer predicting a human's typing they were able to simulate a human typing. The computer made typing errors and had to go back and fix them as well as being run through a ton of different typing challenges. I found this interesting because of the way they tested the computer and the computer was able to realize it made typos and went back and fixed them.
